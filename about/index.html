Victory Projects is an independent organisation that develops and circulates lens-based art exhibitions internationally.

We're based in Lisbon, Portugal, a city in rapid transformation. As both a growing international tech hub and a nexus for postcolonial dialogue, the city has an unusually rich relationship with questions of culture, capital, and representation. It shapes how we think about the work we circulate.

Team
Anne James
Executive Director & Founding Curator

Anne James serves on the board of PhotoIreland, one of Europe's most significant photography organisations. In 2025 PhotoIreland opened the International Centre for the Image in Dublin, consolidating over a decade of work promoting lens-based arts and publishing as a founding member of the EU's Futures and Parallel photography platforms.

James holds an MA in Art and Research Collaboration from Dún Laoghaire Institute of Art, Design and Technology, an MSc in History of Material Culture from the University of Edinburgh, and a BA in Cultural Anthropology from Boston University.

Jaki Gerulskis
Founding Producer

Jaki Gerulskis is Co-Founder of the Lisbon Street Photo Fest, an international photography festival held annually in the Portuguese capital with partners including Fujifilm and the Faculty of Fine Arts at the University of Lisbon. He also co-founded the Lisboa Photo Community (LxPC), an active photography and social community with over 1,000 members. Before founding Victory Projects, he spent a decade producing events and exhibitions at fine arts venues in New England.
