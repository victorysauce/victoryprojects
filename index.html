Victory Projects is an independent, non-profit organisation that produces and circulates high-calibre photography exhibitions internationally. We work with artists at every stage — from initial concept through to touring logistics, catalogue production, and venue relationships.

Our programme is built around work that demands to travel: bodies of photography with genuine ambition, which deserve audiences beyond the city where they were first shown. We are based in Lisbon and operate across Europe, the Americas, and Asia.

We believe the exhibition is itself an art form. The framing, sequencing, and physical encounter with photographs in space is where meaning is made — and that is where we put our energy.
